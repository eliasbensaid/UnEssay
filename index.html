A Story
About Language

On April 25, 2024, students set up tents on Royce Quad at UCLA. Eight days later, riot police arrested over 200 of them. This site is about what happened in between. Not in terms of what physically happened, but in terms of the language used. It is about the words that made one image seem to require the other.

April 25, 2024: Encampment established, Royce Quad
May 1, 2024: Counterprotest attack. Police stand by.
May 2, 2024: Riot police clear encampment. 200+ arrested.
April 25, 2024: Encampment established, Royce Quad
May 2, 2024: Riot police move in. 200+ arrested.

The Central Question

What words traveled between those two images? What did they make possible?
